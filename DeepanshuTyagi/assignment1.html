NATURE


Nature, with all its beauty and grace,
A symphony of sights and sounds to embrace.
Mountains reach high, valleys dip low,
Forests full of life, rivers flow.

The sun rises, painting the sky with hues,
Of gold, pink, and orange, nature renews.
Birds sing sweet songs, bees buzz with glee,
While the world awakens, as far as the eye can see.

Nature is a teacher, with lessons to impart,
Of resilience, renewal, and the power of the heart.
So let us cherish, and protect it with care,
For it is the essence of life, and always will be, a rare.
