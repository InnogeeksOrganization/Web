FRIENDSHIP

:The most purest bond

FRIENDSHIP in simple words is a relationship you have with your chosen family.Friends are the sisters and brothers we never had,and are by our side through life's ups and downs.It is the bonding which becomes so special for us.Unknown person meet and becomes good friends.Each friend represent a world in us,a world probably not born until they arrive.True Friendship multiplies the good in life and divides its evils.A good friend always helps in your bad time.Life is partly what we make it,and partly what it is made by friends we choose.So,Find a group of people who challenge ,you inspire you,and spend a lot of time with them,and it will change your life.
